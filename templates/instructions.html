Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Photo and Video output?

Photo: Exports a static PNG image of the cleaned tweet card with your chosen background, perfect for Instagram posts or static content.

Video: Exports a 1080Ã—1920 MP4 file that combines both the tweet card and any attached video clip into a single reel-ready video for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts.

Which background should I choose?

White: Clean, professional look with high contrast, ideal for maximum readability.

Blur: Uses the video as a soft, dynamic backdrop for a more professional, cinematic feel.

Black: Perfect for dark mode interfaces and OLED displays, offering a sleek, modern appearance that's easy on the eyes.

Can I change the layout after processing?

Absolutely! You can re-process the same tweet with different options anytime. We recommend trying both Video top and Video bottom layouts to see which works best for your specific content and audience.

Does the tweet need to be public?

Yes, only public tweets can be processed. Private, protected, or deleted posts cannot be accessed by our service. Make sure the tweet is publicly viewable before submitting the URL.

How does the estimated time remaining work?

Before processing begins, we try to calculate an estimate for processing time. Photos should always take ~7 seconds to finish, but video processing time varies depending on video length. We provide an estimate, the actual duration might be slightly shorter or longer.
